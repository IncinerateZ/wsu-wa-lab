Korean Movies

Squid Game

Squid Game is a South Korean survival drama television series for Netflix revolving around 456 players competing for 45.6 Billion Won, released on September 17 2021 gaining international attention.

Parasite

Parasite is a 2019 South Korean black comedy thriller film which follows a poor family who scheme to become employed by a wealthy family and infiltrate their household by posing as unrelated, highly qualified individuals. The film is considered by many to be the best film of 2019 winning multiple awards.

Russian Movies

Moscow Doesn't Believe In Tears

Moscow Does Not Believe In Tears is a 1980 Soviet romantic drama film around three young women who come to Moscow from smaller towns and became friends. The film won the Academy Awards for Best Foreign Language Film in 1981.

Mirror

Mirror is a 1975 Russian drama film revolving around memories recalled by a dying poet of key moments in his life and in Soviet culture. The film has grown in reputation since its release and is now considered one of the greatest films of all time.
